About Me

Hi There! My name is Rick Fahey.

Ever since I was I young, I have always tried to understand how things work – even if it meant taking those things apart! And while I enjoyed playing video games, I found myself more intrigued with how the games actually worked. This passion for understanding the “how” led me to application development.

I am currently working on projects involving Full Stack Web development as part of the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp (RCB) curriculum. Please take a look at my portfolio of projects to see how I'm making use of this technology.

I also love the outdoors and play all types of individual and team sports. Throughout my journey, I learned the importance of planning, preparation and putting trust in the team. So whether it was cave diving off Roatán’s coast or hiking the mountains of Utah, I became a student of the sport and immersed myself it in completely!

I have now taken the same ambition and interest in continually improving my application development skills at RCB by working with talented individuals and faculty. I have happily turned my favorite hobby into a new career proposition!
